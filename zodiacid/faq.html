About Our Astrology Reports

What exactly do I get?

A 40+ page PDF natal chart report analyzing your complete birth chart. Covers zodiac sign personality traits, emotions, communication, relationships, career, and 7 more life areas. Includes your natal wheel chart visualization. Delivered to your email.

How are the reports created?

We compute your exact birth chart using Astrolog 7.80, a professional astronomical engine built on Swiss Ephemeris. This calculates positions for 20 celestial bodies, all aspect configurations with precise orbs, and 12 Placidus house placements. Our interpretation system then analyzes each configuration through 13 specialized modules grounded in the traditions of psychological astrology (Liz Greene), aspect analysis (Robert Hand), and evolutionary astrology (Steven Forrest).

What makes ZodiacID different from other birth chart reading sites?

Most services use only your Sun sign or brief template paragraphs. We analyze 20 celestial bodies (including 5 asteroids and 5 sensitive points), 9 aspect types (including minors like semi-square and quincunx), and interpret each aspect with its exact orb. The natal report alone is over 40 pages of original analysis. Written natively in 3 languages — not translated.

Are these reports scientifically proven?

No. Astrology is not a science. We don’t predict the future or make factual claims. Our reports offer a structured framework for self-exploration based on your birth chart — not predictions.

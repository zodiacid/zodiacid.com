About ZodiacID

Astronomical precision meets psychological interpretation.

The Story

ZodiacID started with a question: what if natal chart readings had the same precision as the astronomical calculations behind them?

Most birth chart readings rely on generic Sun-sign descriptions or brief template paragraphs. We took a different approach. Using Astrolog 7.80 — a professional astronomical engine built on Swiss Ephemeris — we compute exact positions for 20 celestial bodies, map all 9 aspect types with precise orbs down to the arcminute, and calculate full Placidus house placements.

Then we apply an interpretation methodology grounded in the work of Liz Greene (psychological astrology), Robert Hand (systematic aspect analysis), and Steven Forrest (evolutionary astrology). The result is a 40+ page report that reads like it was written by someone who actually studied your chart — because our system was designed to do exactly that.

Our Approach

Every report starts with your exact birth data — date, time, and city. We calculate the precise position of each celestial body at your moment of birth. No two charts are alike, and no two reports are alike.

Our analysis covers 13 specialized modules: from core identity (Sun sign, Ascendant) through emotional patterns (Moon sign, 4th house) to career strengths (10th house, Saturn aspects) and love compatibility (Venus, Mars, 7th house). Each module interprets your specific zodiac sign placements — not generic horoscope descriptions.

Methodology

Our reports are based on natal chart data calculated by Astrolog 7.80, a precise astronomical engine used by professional astrologers worldwide. The interpretation methodology draws from decades of established astrological frameworks — psychological, evolutionary, and humanistic traditions.

Astrology is not a science, and we don’t pretend otherwise. We don’t predict the future or claim certainty. What we offer is a framework for exploring personality patterns and life themes through the lens of your birth chart.

Founder

Founded by Vadik Iljin in Tallinn, Estonia. Background in data analysis and pattern recognition. The kind of person who sees a birth chart and thinks “what if I systematized this?”

Small operation, big attention to detail. Every report receives personal review before delivery.

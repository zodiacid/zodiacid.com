How It Works

  1. Enter Your Birth Details

    Full name, date of birth, exact time, and city of birth. The more precise, the more detailed the report.

    Astrolog 7.80 (Swiss Ephemeris) calculates exact positions for 20 celestial bodies — 10 classical planets, 5 asteroids (Chiron, Ceres, Pallas, Juno, Vesta), and 5 sensitive points (Nodes, Lilith, Vertex, Part of Fortune). The analysis builds sequentially — each section references what came before, so it reads as one coherent narrative.

    Every report is proofread and checked for coherence, accuracy, and depth. PDF sent to your email within 24 hours.

Questions About the Natal Report

What’s in a natal report?

A 40+ page analysis of your birth chart, covering 13 life areas: personality, emotions, communication, love, career, growth, discipline, change, intuition, power, healing, life patterns, and a final synthesis. Every section is based on the exact positions of 20 celestial bodies at the moment and location of your birth.

How long is it?

Over 40 pages — roughly 11,000 words. This is not a quick summary; it’s a thorough exploration of your chart. Most people read it in stages, returning to different sections as life situations arise.

Is this specific to me?

Yes. The report is based on the exact positions of 20 celestial bodies at your precise birth time and location. No two charts are the same, and no two reports are the same. This is not a generic Sun sign description — it reflects the full complexity of your unique chart.

Yes. Reports are available in English, Russian, and Estonian. Each language version is written natively — not translated. Choose your language in the order form above.

What exactly do I get?

A 40+ page PDF natal chart report covering 12 life areas — personality traits, love patterns, career strengths, and more. Includes your natal wheel chart. Delivered directly to your email.

How are the reports created?

We manually process your birth data through a multi-stage pipeline. First, Astrolog 7.80 (Swiss Ephemeris) computes exact positions for 20 celestial bodies and maps 50+ aspect configurations with precise orbs. We then extract your chart’s unique statistical signature — planetary weights, elemental distribution, aspect geometry, and dispositorship chains. Each of the 13 report sections is then written individually for your placements, using the interpretive frameworks of Liz Greene (psychological astrology), Robert Hand (systematic aspect analysis), and Steven Forrest (evolutionary astrology). Every report is written, proofread, and checked by hand before delivery.

Are these reports scientifically proven?

No. Astrology is not a science. We don’t predict the future — we offer a framework for self-exploration based on your birth chart.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

Check your birth certificate or ask a family member. An approximate time usually works, though exact time gives the most detailed results.

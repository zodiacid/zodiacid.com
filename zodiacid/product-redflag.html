Questions About the Red Flag Check

What’s a Red Flag Check?

A focused analysis of the hardest aspects between two birth charts. Instead of covering every relationship theme, we zoom in on friction: the top reasons you’ll argue, realistic conflict scenarios based on your chart dynamics, and practical scripts for navigating those moments. Short, direct, and honest.

How is this different from the Relationship X-Ray?

The Relationship X-Ray is a comprehensive synastry report covering chemistry, communication, growth, and conflict across all aspects. The Red Flag Check is narrower and cheaper — it focuses only on friction points and conflict patterns. Think of it as the “what will we fight about” report. If you want the full picture, get the Relationship X-Ray. If you want a quick, honest look at the hard parts, this is it.

Is it really that honest?

Yes. The Red Flag Check doesn’t soften the hard aspects or add disclaimers to every observation. If your charts show a tendency toward power struggles, we name it. If there’s a pattern suggesting emotional manipulation, we describe how it tends to play out. The survival scripts are there to help — but the analysis itself doesn’t sugar-coat anything.

Questions About Synastry

What is synastry?

Synastry is the comparison of two birth charts to explore relationship dynamics. By overlaying two charts, we can see how one person’s planets interact with the other’s — where there’s natural harmony, where friction tends to build, and where you help each other grow. It’s not about compatibility scores or pass/fail judgments. It’s a map of the relationship’s themes and tendencies.

Do we need exact birth times?

Exact birth times produce the most detailed analysis, especially for house placements and the Ascendant. If you have approximate times (within an hour or two), the report will still be thorough — the core planetary aspects between two charts remain the same. Moon sign may shift if the birth time is off by several hours, so check birth certificates when possible.

What aspects do you analyze?

We analyze 9 aspect types: Conjunction, Sextile, Square, Trine, Opposition, Semi-Square, Sesquiquadrate, Quincunx, and Semi-Sextile. Every cross-aspect between the two charts is identified with exact orb and interpreted in the context of the relationship. Hard aspects (Square, Opposition) indicate friction and growth. Soft aspects (Trine, Sextile) indicate ease and natural flow.
